Escape Room Game

You have been locked in an escape room with no choice but to find hidden clues that will lead to your escape. Step into different immersive rooms, where each object holds a clue to unlock the secrets of the house. With secret doors and rooms, can you even trust the walls? Decode the hidden messages to move forward and enter the final, high-stakes room, where you must hack into a computer to escape. Along the way, you’ll be given important cyber security tips to stay safe while solving the intricate puzzles. Can you outsmart the gamemaster and escape in time? Dive into this immersive experience and find out!

User guide

Press the green flag on the top left corner to start the game. press ‘X’ on your keyboard to continue playing. Then press ‘E’ on your keyboard to continue to the first room of the game
Interact with objects and click on them to find letters!
When all objects are clicked and the letters are put together, you should be able to unscramble them to form the word ‘safe’. If you then type this word into the answer box and click enter on your keyboard or click the tick button with your cursor, the screen will switch to the next room.
